Frequently Asked Questions
How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery within Cairo takes 1–2 business days. Other governorates typically receive orders within 3–5 business days.
Can I return a product?
Yes, unopened products in original packaging can be returned within 14 days of purchase. Prescription medicines are non-returnable.
Do you require a prescription?
Certain regulated medications require a valid prescription. You can upload your prescription during checkout or email it to us.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept cash on delivery, Visa, Mastercard, and Vodafone Cash. All online transactions are secured with SSL encryption.
How do I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive an SMS and email with a tracking link. You can also view your order status under My Orders.
